Powerful Features for Modern Teams

Everything you need to collaborate effectively, all in one platform

Document Creation & Editing

Create rich text documents with real-time editing capabilities. Format text, add images, and collaborate seamlessly with your team.

Interactive Whiteboards

Design, brainstorm, and visualize ideas on infinite digital whiteboards. Perfect for planning, wireframing, and creative sessions.

Permission Management

Control who can view, edit, or comment on your documents. Set permissions at the document level for secure collaboration.

Real-time Chat

Chat with team members directly within each document. No need to switch between apps - everything is integrated.

Version History

Track all changes with detailed version history. See who made what changes and when, with the ability to restore previous versions.

User Presence

See who's online and actively working on documents in real-time. Cursors and selections are visible to all collaborators.
